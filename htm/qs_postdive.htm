To stop CoPilot Sonar, move your mouse to the top of the CoPilot Sonar window to reveal the window controls and click on the red X in the upper left to close the CoPilot Sonar window. Then, click on the X in the center section to close the operating environment.

There are no specific post dive requirements for CoPilot sonar, but you should follow the post dive requirements for the VideoRay Pro 4 and BlueView sonar.
